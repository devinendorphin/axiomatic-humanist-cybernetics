  ahc-verified-kernel/
      Lean 4 project: 4 modules, 77 audited theorems, zero sorries,
      core-only. v0.4 implements the four constitutional dispositions
      ratified by the project owner on 2026-07-12, as proposed by the
      Phase 2 external review (docs/external-reviews/):

      Ruling D-R1A (with D-R4) — Two Clocks. The hazard clock and the
        epistemic clock are separated. Persistence of threshold
        exceedance sustains only a constrained CONTINUITY-HOLD floor
        (deployment-certified, reversible, Tier-1-capped, Layer 0 review
        owed); only an attested materially new claim restarts the full
        72h PIO. Episode machine theorems E1-E11 replace v0.3's
        R-family: whatever the exceedance pattern, unconfirmed full
        protection never exceeds 72 hours per evidentiary episode (E1),
        the floor is never withdrawn under continuing risk (E5), and
        continuity of a signal is never continuity of the full
        authority (E8).

      Rulings D-R2A + D-R3 — Certified Reversibility Envelopes. The
        scalar routing bound is repealed. Routing AND severance (Q2 of
        the D-2 review: answered YES) are irreversibility-bearing unless
        a deployment certificate places the described action inside a
        demonstrated-reversible envelope; reversibility is judged
        against affected human and institutional state, not machine
        topology. Envelope theorems W1-W9 replace v0.3's V-family:
        certified routing keeps Tier 1, certified soft severance keeps
        Tier 2, everything uncertified requires Tier-3 causal
        identification (W5a/W5b), the mechanism table remains a floor
        certificates can only raise (W7), the absence of a certificate
        is never evidence of reversibility (W8), and the zero envelope
        is a legal parameterization (W9 - bound_pos repealed).
