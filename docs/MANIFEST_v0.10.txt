      ratification): an unreviewed hold under continuing risk lands in

      throughout — a review breach is never a protection vacuum (E5
      revised as floor_persists; E15). E1/E2 are STRENGTHENED: the
      72-hour bound now holds across close and continue dispositions,

      bounds carry the corresponding hypothesis updates.
