WHAT THE MAP DOES AND DOES NOT COVER
  The map quotes the exact sentences the Phase 1 theorem families
  formalize. The v0.4+ theorem families that formalize CONSTITUTIONAL
  RULINGS from the review rounds (D-R1A, D-R2A, D-R3, D-R4) and the
  Reviewer #2 repairs (v0.8-v0.12) are mapped to the amendment record
  (ERRATA_AND_AMENDMENTS.md) and the archived review exchange, not to
  the v3.1 text: those constructions are post-text constitutional
  process, and presenting them as v3.1 sentences would overstate the
  text. Where the kernel renders a spec quantity in different units or
  supplies a constant the text does not fix, the map says so.
